Instructions

Intro

Taboo online is a game that brings the classic taboo game to teams remotely. So, it is best played in a video call, such as Zoom. You will need to talk to your teammates when it is your turn as the clue-giver!

Gameplay

You will choose to be one of two teams. Try to make the team as even as possible.

Teams will take turns either guessing or listening the other team. The guessing team guesses the word, while the opposing team ensures that no taboo word was said.

The team with the most points after set amount the rounds wins!

Your turn

As the clue-giver, you will have to describe the highlighted top word on the card by talking, without saying any of the taboo words. In this example, you want your team to guess the word 'jog', but you cannot say the words jog, run, walk, fast, morning, or path. You could say something like "A light sprint".

You can skip a card by pressing 'Skip'

Guess

If your teammates is the clue-giver, you guess the word by typing into the text box.

Listen

If the other team is guessing, listen to the clue-giver. If they say any words on the list, click the 'Said it!' button.

Have fun!
